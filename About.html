About - TOUR BUDDY !

Tour Buddy is a web application that helps users to create geographic routes all around the world from the places registered at Foursquare. User's can create routes and save them along with description and pictures. Users can not only save their routes but also see the directions for the specified route with different modes of travel like Bicycle, Walking and Driving. Other users can shop for these routes and use them when on a expedition. This app helps users keep track of their travel and lets them share their favorite routes with their family and friends.
